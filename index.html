About ConstiConnect

"WE, THE PEOPLE OF INDIA, having solemnly resolved to constitute India into a SOVEREIGN SOCIALIST SECULAR DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC and to secure to all its citizens..."
— Preamble to the Constitution of India

Our Mission

ConstiConnect bridges the gap between current events and constitutional knowledge. Using advanced NLP and AI technologies, we help citizens understand how daily news relates to the fundamental principles enshrined in the Indian Constitution.

AI Technology

Our platform uses Python-based machine learning models to analyze news content and match it with relevant constitutional provisions. The system employs TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity to identify connections between news text and constitutional articles.

Constitutional Dataset

We maintain a comprehensive database of constitutional articles with detailed explanations, keywords, and categories. This dataset is continuously updated and refined to improve the accuracy of our AI matching system.

Why It Matters

In a democracy, informed citizenship is crucial. By connecting news with constitutional articles, we promote legal awareness, civic education, and democratic participation. Every citizen deserves to understand their rights and duties as outlined in our Constitution.
